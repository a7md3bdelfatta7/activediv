ABOUT US

Who We Are

ACTIVDEV is a Web Solutions Engineering Agency located in Egypt. This is one of the best offshore locations to outsource your Web & Mobile needs.

Our main focus is saving your time and efforts by providing full-cycle product development from gathering your requirements, designing, coding, testing, documenting, and finally maintaining, all with high-quality, cost-effective solutions under any constraints agreed upon.

What We Do

We provide cost-effective Web & Mobile solutions for startups, shops and companies. We are experts in web & Mobile design, Front-end development, Back-end development, Databases, as well as dealing with the most frameworks and software.

  • CMS Websites.
  • E-Commerces.
  • Web Applications.
  • Mobile Apllications (Android & Ios)
  • Landing Pages.
  • Blogs & Social Media.
