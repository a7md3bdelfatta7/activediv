ABOUT US

Who We Are

ACTIVDEV presents Web & Mobile Solutions that are creative, highly flexible, cost-effective located in Cairo Egypt,

Our mission is providing you with a high-quality and cost-effective Web & Mobile Solutions Starting from collecting requirements, designing, coding, testing, documenting, deployment and finally maintaining.

What We Do

We provide cost-effective and high-quality Web & Mobile solutions for startups, shops and companies. We are experts in web & Mobile design, Front-end development, Back-end development, Databases, as well as dealing with the latest frameworks and technologies.
